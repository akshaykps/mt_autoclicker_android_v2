# MT Auto Clicker for Android — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 19, 2026

MT Auto Clicker for Android is published by WebTreta. This policy explains the
data used by the Android app identified by package
`net.mtautoclicker.android`.

## Accessibility and screen automation

MT Auto Clicker uses Android AccessibilityService only after you review an
in-app disclosure and enable the service in Android settings.

The service can perform taps, swipes, holds, and Back/Home/Recents actions that
you explicitly configure. During macro recording, it can inspect the active
window and focused editable field to capture actions you request. This
Accessibility content is processed on your device. It is not uploaded, sold,
shared, or used for advertising.

The app also requests “Display over other apps” access to show its floating
control bar and target markers.

## Optional usage analytics

Usage analytics are disabled by default. If you opt in, the app may send:

- a random app-generated Device ID;
- app version and installation source;
- Android version/SDK, CPU architecture, device manufacturer/brand/model;
- language and timezone;
- app opens, feature-use events, and interactions with in-app tours or messages.

Our server may derive an approximate country from the request IP. Analytics are
used to understand reliability and feature usage. You can disable analytics at
any time in Settings. Disabling analytics removes queued analytics that have not
yet been uploaded.

## Feedback, support and notifications

If you submit feedback, we receive the message, optional rating, Device ID, and
basic app/device metadata needed to diagnose the issue and deliver a support
reply. When you read or dismiss an in-app notification, the app may send the
notification action and Device ID so the inbox can stay synchronized.

## Local data

Presets, recent runs, macros, settings, and cached guide content are stored on
your device. The app provides export, deletion and factory-reset controls.
Android cloud backup is disabled for the app.

## Data sharing and advertising

The Android app contains no advertising SDK and does not sell personal data.
Data is sent over HTTPS to MT Auto Clicker's backend and infrastructure
providers only as needed to operate the features described above.

## Retention and deletion

Local data remains until you delete it, reset the app, or uninstall it. To
request deletion of server-side Android data, submit a request at
https://www.mtautoclicker.com/feedback-form/ and include the Device ID displayed
in the app's Settings screen so we can identify the records.

## Children

The app is a general-purpose utility and is not directed to children. Users
must only automate actions they are authorized to perform.

## Changes

We may update this policy when the app or legal requirements change. The
effective date above will be updated when material changes are published.

## Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, use:
